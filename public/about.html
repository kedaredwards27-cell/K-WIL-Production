Who We Are

At K Wil Production we specialize in creating powerful live sound and polished recordings. From front-of-house (FOH) systems to backline rentals, track composition, vocal arrangements and label services — our team delivers professional results for artists and events.

Our Services

  • FOH Rentals: Main speakers, subwoofers & mixing consoles tailored to your crowd size.
  • Backline Rentals: Drum kits, amps, keyboards, mic packs and artist-ready rigs.
  • Mix & Mastering: Professional polishing for release-ready tracks.
  • Track & Vocal Arrangements: Composition, arrangement, harmonies and production.
  • Record Label Support: Artist development, production and distribution guidance.
